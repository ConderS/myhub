In the admin dashboard for your website: click the DNS section.

1. Look for the NS (name server) records

2. Copy them

3. Go to domain: MANAGE (Edit, etc..)

4. Change Nameservers for the domain

5. Choose the customization option and PASTE the name servers into the options. 

6. DONE :D


Check functionality by going to the website domain through your browser.

It works if there's an error page that has the Hosting service on it. 

(Or if the webpage actually loads)
